Luc's Ascii tower

This tower game was built by three people.

The first thing We did was talking about who does what and how we are going to achieve our goals.

My task was to do the third and fourth level which I've made within 6 hours.

The tower has six levels and a lot of hard to complete tasks which only teacher could do.

I made a part where the console gives you questions and the user needed to answer them.

The only problem with that was that almost nobody knew so much about computer's that they couldn't answer the questions.

The other thing I made was a chamber where luc's kid was, and his goal was to kill you in the most unique ways.

Like one time he drops a nuke and the console just shuts down.

After we were done we talked about the project and asked for feedback from teachers, and they were pretty positive.
